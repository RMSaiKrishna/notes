Audio & Text Notes • Private by design

About this application

A minimal, mobile‑friendly note keeper that lets you save voice memos and text notes directly in your browser using IndexedDB and Blob storage. Your data stays on your device unless you export it.

Why you'll love it

  • Record clear audio notes with one tap.
  • Type quick text notes with timestamps.
  • Works offline; everything is stored locally.
  • Export notes as files whenever you want.

Privacy & storage

We use your browser's IndexedDB to store note data as Blobs. Nothing leaves your device unless you choose to share or export.

IndexedDBBlobPWA‑ready

How it works

  1. Tap the microphone to start/stop recording. Audio is saved as a Blob.
  2. Give your note a title and optional description.
  3. Everything is saved locally. You can export or share any note via link.

Ready to try it? It takes less than 10 seconds to create your first note. No sign‑up needed.
